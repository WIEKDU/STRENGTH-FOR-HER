To brainstorm new ideas on,

“Strength for Her” is a live panel discussion that will give women the strength to build a women's network filled with entertainment and surprising giveaways. This will be important in promoting young female undergraduates’ sense of self-worth, their ability to determine their own choices, and their right to influence social change for themselves and others. And we are planning to organise small stalls in the area near the auditorium and the first-floor open area to encourage female undergraduates.

SPEAKER 03

Ms. Nadeesha Fernando

Ms. Nadeesha Fernando is a HR professional with 15yrs of experience in various industries such as Telco, IT and BPM. She has qualified in both HR and Law and has exposure in all the HR functions. She has been working as a guest lecturer for multiple universities such as university of Colombo, Japura, Sabaragamuwa etc and has a special interest in grooming the younger generation. She is currently serving in the capacity of Manager - HR for Virtusa SL, as Head of Legal and Compliance for the Location, and handling multiple areas overlooking about 700+ employees. Her hobbies include reading, travelling and spending time in the nature.
